Overview

Fashion is a form of self-expression that reflects cultural trends, personal identitiy, and creativity through clothing, accessories, hairstyles, and makeup. It evolves over time, influenced by society, art technology, and individual style. Fashion combines creativity with technology to design clothing and acessories. Technology is transforming how fashion is made, worn, and experienced.
