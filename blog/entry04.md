## Context
In the past we focused on our topics technology and inventions. This time we had to choose a coding website that will be used for our topic. My topic is fashion and I chose the website Aframe. Aframe is a website where you can make a 3d model of anything on your computer. I did research before choosing Aframe and I also tinkered with it. I used a starter template from the aframe website and copied it onto jsbin where I did my tinkering. The starting template consisted of a flat plane with a box, cylinder, and shpere on it. I tinkered by removing the box, changing colors and duplicating shapes. I made what could be a demo of a possible fashion invention. The tinkering I did was to make sure I understood Aframe well to use in my final freedom project.
